War Rules

War Rules

Individual Responsibility
  1. ​Ensure that your base complies with the FWA Base Requirement.
  2. Report to the Management if any war base does not comply with FWA Base Requirement.
Instructions to Attack
  1. Maximum Stars allowed per Attack
    • ​​Win Lotto: 3 STAR (NO EXCEPTION PLEASE)
    • Loose Lotto: 2 STAR or 1 STAR (IF your attack would exceed 80 STARS)
      ​
  2. Attack Sequence
    1. ​1st attack: Your Mirror
      (e.g. Your War Position is #12, Attack Opponent's #12)

      WITH EFFECT of 12 October 2017, it is mandatory for you to utilise your 1st attack in yielding stars and Clan XP for the clan.

      You are to: Attack Your Mirror; OR ONLY       IF your Mirror is attacked due to the Last Twelve Hours Exception Base rule (below), attack any Unattacked Base.

      IF you do not       abide to this rule in multiple occasions, you will be opted-out for five (5) wars as punishment.

    2. 2nd attack: See below (Choose one out of three)

      Any Attacked Bases
      You may attack any bases which have been attack, provided that you do not exceed the "Maximum Stars allowed per Attack" rule (see above).

      Mutually Agreed Bases
      You may attack other clan member's mirror, provided mutual agreement was made beforehand. You are required to seek permission every war unless agreed mutually otherwise.

      Last Twelve Hour Exception Bases
      You may attack any unattacked bases at the last twelve hours of battle day.

  3. Overall Stars Threshold
    1. ​40/40 War
      • ​Win Lotto: 120 Stars (All Bases 3 Starred)
      • Loose Lotto: Maximum 79 Stars

  4. ​​War (Opt-Out) Priority for non-40/40 wars (in sequence)
    1. Bases that have failed the audit or are not audited
    2. Elders and Members who are away from Game (and those who Opt-Out)
    3. Leaders who are away from Game
    4. ​Those who violated War Rule for previous war
    5. Those who did not attack for previous war
      1. ​Those who did not attack at all
      2. Those who use one​ out of two attacks

Note:
  • Clan members whom fail to adhere to the "Instructions to Attack" rule may be opted out in future wars.
  • Clan members whom fails to attack for 2 consecutive wars will be kicked.
  • Subjected to follow both Clan and Alliance Rules violation mechanism.
