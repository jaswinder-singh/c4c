Alliance Rules

Farm War ALLIANCE (FWA) Rules

Attack Methods & Star Threshold
  1. Refer to War Rules.

War Reinforcement Troops
  1. NO reinforcement troops should be donated in the War Clan Castle in any circumstances unless informed otherwise by the Management.

Base Requirement
3 STAR-able Base
  1. Town Hall (must be at the Edge), 9 Storages and other Non-Attack Buildings are to be placed together
    Note: Air Defence and Air Sweeper are considered as non-attack building​​
  2. Eagle Artillery on the edge next to Town Hall
  3. Skeleton Traps set to Air (Bat Wings)
  4. Walls not to block Giant Deployment within the Air Defence region
  5. Air Defence region requires one side exposed minimum (for troop deployment)
  6. Infernos on Single Target; X-Bow on Ground Target

Land and Air Defence Region
  1. ​Segregate Land and Air Defences away from each other
    • ​Land: Mortars, Cannons, X-Bows [Set to Land target only] and Barbarian King
    • Air: Archer Towers, Wizard Towers, Inferno Towers [Set to Single target only], Hidden Teslas and Archer Queen

Penalties

Unlike the Clan Rules, Farm War Alliance enforces stringent rules and regulations to ensure fairness to other war clans. The universal violation mechanism would be:

  • Kicked and Ban from Clan and/or all Farm War Alliance Clans
​
Note: Management reserve the rights to bypass this violation mechanism, as deemed fit, without any prior notice or explanation.
