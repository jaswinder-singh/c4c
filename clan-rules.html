Clan Rules

General Clan Rules

Activeness
  1. This clan only accepts and retains members whom are active in the game.
  2. Active members are determined based on the donation count and war activity.
  3. Inactive members will be kicked; but are welcome back when they are active.

Donation Requirement
  1. This refers to the amount of Troops received and donated per season.
  2. You are encouraged to donate according to the request message.
  3. 3000 or 1:3 (Donate:Receive) ratio per season (Whichever is higher).
  4. The number of donations will determine war priority (see War Rules for priority) for non-50/50 syncs.

War Rules
  1. This refers to the regulations imposed when in War.
  2. All members are required to adhere to the rules stated in the Farm War Alliance Rules and War Rules page.

Away from Game
  1. Members who are away from the game are required to:
    1. Inform the Management
    2. Opt-Out

Leaving Clan
  1. Members may leave the clan for not more than twice a month to visit other clans. They are to inform a Leader or Elder in order to be allowed back and are to return within 4 hours. Members status (e.g. Eldership/Co-Leadership) will be reinstated accordingly and are subjected to regular Donation Counts.

  2. Members may leave the clan permanently in good faith by informing the Management. They will be allowed back provided prior notification was given to theManagement.
    ​
  3. Members who leaves the clan without good faith (a courtesy message) will be barred from returning the clan.

Dispute Mechanism
  1. Contact the Management for mediation.
  2. Unauthorised use of power to resolve a dispute internally is prohibited.

Disclaimer
  1. Directions issued by any Management Leader will supersede any rules stated in this page.

CLan/Group Chat Rules

Standard Rules
  1. Please refrain from cursing or swearing.
  2. Discriminatory or hate speech will not be tolerated.

Band & Discord App
  1. Please identify yourself when you first join the group.
  2. For Band group, please set your Band Profile name as your Village (In-Game) Name and leave "Whats on your mind?" field empty.

Penalties

The following violation mechanism is used to ensure a fair chance is given to all players (especially if it is a honest mistake):
  • ​1st time: Warning &/OR Attack ONLY at LAST Four Hours for next War Sync
  • 2nd time: Demotion / Kick and Ban from Clan
  • 3rd time: Kick and Ban from Clan
​
Note: Player violation records will reset at the end of each season. Management reserve the rights to bypass this violation mechanism, as deemed fit, without any prior notice or explanation.
