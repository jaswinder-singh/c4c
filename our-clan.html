About Our Clan

Who we are, What we do

Clash For Cash is a Level 5 clan. We started our journey as a war clan (W:51 L:7 D:1 with a 20 war win streak - proof) then transitioned into a FWA clan. We are a new English-speaking war-loot clan and are proud to be recognised as a member of Farm War Alliance (FWA).
Being part of Farm War Alliance (FWA), we will start our War simultaneously with over 460 registered clans in order to increase the chances of matching with one and another.

Upon successful sync, the clan with the most number of losses (in the past seven wars) will be the winner of the war.
